Directions/Map

We are conveniently located in the Downingtown professional Plaza

797 East Lancaster Ave. Suite 10 Downingtown, PA 19335

Plenty of free parking. In addition to our office waiting area there’s also a beautiful courtyard that you may access. Our outside office area is well lit for evening appointments.

If you prefer not to wait in the office or have errands to run while our child is being evaluated, there are many conveniences nearby our office such as Wawa, Chick-fil-A, PJ Whelihan’s, Hibachi, Pomodoro, and Estrada restaurants, and Wegman’s grocery store. We are also close to Home Depot, BJ’s Whole Foods, Petsmart, Best Buy, Downingtown Regal Cinema, L.A. Fitness, and the Downingtown country Club.

We are easily accessible by train via septa and the R-5 train line see www.septa.org for schedule. We are accessible from the Thorndale train station, Downingtown train station, Swedesford Road and Exton train stations.
