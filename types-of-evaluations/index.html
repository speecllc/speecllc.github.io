Types of Evaluations

    All psycho-educational evaluations include:
  1. an intake session with parent/guardian
  2. all testing sessions needed
  3. review of records and data collection, and
  4. a feedback session with parents/guardians to review results.

High School/College Testing
High School College Testing is typically completed for students who currently have IEP’s, during the student’s senior year as part of preparation for transition to college. Please check with the student’s college/university to obtain their requirements prior to scheduling an evaluation, otherwise, the standard cognitive and achievement battery will be administered and acceptance of the evaluation by the college/university is at their discretion.
