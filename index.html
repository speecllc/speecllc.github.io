  • Limited Evaluations

    Don’t necessarily need a full psycho-educational evaluation and are only looking for a cognitive assessment or an evaluation for achievement; behavior or rating scales for ADHD or executive functions. We provide limited evaluations and can tailor them to meet your needs.

  • College Testing for Accommodations

    Many colleges and universities require updated testing in the area of your child’s disability in order to determine if they are eligible for accommodations in college and what type of accommodations are needed. We are happy to assist you providing the appropriate evaluation based on the requirements of the college or university your child will be attending.

  • Private School Admission

    Many private schools require a cognitive ability assessment as part of their admission requirements. We would be happy to assist you in providing this testing for your child’s entrance into private school.

  • Auxillary Consultative Services

    SPEEC offers speech/language evaluations to assess articulation, receptive, expressive language and pragmatic language needs. We also offer occupational therapy evaluations to address fine motor, visual motor, visual-perceptual, sensory processing needs. Upon request, these auxiliary evaluations can be included as part of the your child’s psycho - educational evaluation or separately as independent evaluations.
